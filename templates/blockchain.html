Blockchain

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a digital system for recording information in a secure and transparent way. It works like a digital ledger or notebook that keeps a record of transactions, but once something is added, it can’t be changed. This makes blockchain reliable and tamper-proof.Learn more

Key Features

  • Decentralized: No one owns it; it's shared across many computers.
  • Secure: Hard to hack or change.
  • Transparent: Everyone in the network can see the records.
  • Permanent: Once added, data stays forever.
    • Learn more

Common Uses

  • Cryptocurrency: Powers Bitcoin and Ethereum.
  • Supply Chain: Tracks products from factory to store.
  • Smart Contracts: Automates agreements.
  • Healthcare: Stores medical records securely.
    • Learn more

Benefits of Blockchain

  • Transparency: Everyone can see the records.
  • Security: Hard to hack or alter.
  • Efficiency: Speeds up processes like payments.
  • Cost-Saving: No need for middlemen, like banks.
